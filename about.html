Allyson is currently the Communications & Product Manager for Norlab USA, the manufacturer and distributor of the Depilar System. She graduated from The University of Southern California in 2011 with a double major in Communication (Entertainment, Media, & Society emphasis), and English Literature. While studying at USC she had her heart set on a career in public relations and/or entertainment. She landed internships at E! Entertainment Television, PMK*BNC, Pierce Mattie Public Relations, and several lifestyle/fashion PR firms.

However, after a brief stint at a beauty PR firm in Los Angeles, the opportunity to work in-house at Norlab USA presented itself and Allyson has been with them ever since.

In her spare time, Allyson enjoys anything related to fashion, beauty, music, wine, and otters.
